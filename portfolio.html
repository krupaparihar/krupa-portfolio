About Me

I am Krupa Parihar from Achalpur, District Amravati, currently pursuing my B.Tech in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at PCCOE, Pune. Coming from a small town and stepping into a competitive academic environment has shaped me into someone who is adaptable, curious, and determined to grow continuously.

I have a strong interest in technology, especially in areas where software and electronics come together. Alongside my technical journey, I also have a creative side — I enjoy cooking, designing, sketching, and expressing ideas through fashion and art. These hobbies help me stay balanced and bring creativity into my problem-solving approach.

I believe in learning by doing, and I actively work on projects involving simulations, embedded systems, and programming. My goal is to build meaningful solutions and continuously grow.
